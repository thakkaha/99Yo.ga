Mantras


ॐ ध्येय सदा सवितृ मण्डलमध्यवर्ति, नारायण सरसिज़ासनसन्निविष्ट ।
केयूरवान मकर कुण्डलवानकिरीटी, हारी हिरण्मय वपूर्धृत शंख चक्र ॥

dhyeya sadaa savitRRi maNDalamadhyavarti, naaraayaNa sarasiज़्aasanasanniviShTa |
keyuuravaana makara kuNDalavaanakiriiTii, haarii hiraNmaya vapuurdhRRita shaMkha chakra ||
  1. om mitrāya namah (ॐ मित्राय नमः)
  2. om ravaye namah (ॐ रवये नमः)
  3. om sūryāya namah (ॐ सूर्याय नमः)
  4. om bhānave namah (ॐ भानवे नमः)
  5. om khagāya namah (ॐ खगाय नमः)
  6. om pushne namah (ॐ पूष्णे नमः)
  7. om hiranya garbhāya namah (ॐ हिरण्यगर्भाय नमः)
  8. om marīcaye namah (ॐ मरीचये नमः)
  9. om ādityāya namah (ॐ आदित्याय नमः)
  10. om savitre namah (ॐ सवित्रे नमः)
  11. om arkāya namah (ॐ अर्काय नमः)
  12. om bhāskarāya namah (ॐ भास्कराय नमः)
om śrīsavitrasūryanārāyaṇāya namaḥ (ॐ श्रीसवित्रसूर्यनारायणाय नमः)

॥ आदित्यस्य नमस्कारन् ये कुर्वन्ति दिने दिने
आयुः प्रज्ञा बलम् वीर्यम् तेजस्तेशान् च जायते ॥

|| aadityasya namaskaaran ye kurvanti dine dine
aayuH praj~naa balam viiryam tejasteshaan cha jaayate ||

Poses ... Scroll to see all steps

  • 1. Exhale while you stand erect with hands folded as in a prayer position.

  • 2. Inhale as you raise your arms over your head and bend backward

  • 3. Exhale as you bend forward to touch the floor with your hands (without bending at knees)

  • 4. Inhale as you send first foot backward and keep the other foot to a lunge position

  • 5. Hold your breadth as your move your other leg backward and place it next to the first one

  • 6. Exhale as you lower your body down and rest on your hands. Keep both feet together. Stomach & knees are off the floor. Hands, chin and toes touch the floor.

  • 7. Inhale as you push the body forward and raise head, shoulder & chest.

  • 8. Exhale as you raise your body and hips. Heels & palms should be flat on the floor.

  • 9. Inhale as you place your first foot between the arms with other foot extended backward

  • 10. Exhale as you bend forward to touch the floor with your hands (without bending at knees)

  • 11. Inhale as you raise your arms over your head and bend backward

  • 12. Exhale while you stand erect with hands folded as in a prayer position.
